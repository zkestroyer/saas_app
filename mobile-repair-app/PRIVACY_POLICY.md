# Privacy Policy

**Revivix — Premium Mobile Repair Network**
*Last Updated: May 14, 2026*

---

## 1. Introduction

Revivix ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- **Account Data**: Name, email address, phone number, role selection
- **Device Information**: Device brand, model, and issue descriptions you provide during repair bookings
- **Photos**: Device damage photos you upload during the booking process

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Identifiers**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App feature usage, screen navigation patterns
- **Push Notification Tokens**: For delivering notifications to your device

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT access your contacts, messages, or personal files
- We do NOT track your location in the background
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Process repair bookings and match you with technicians
- Generate invoices and process payments
- Send push notifications about repair status updates
- Improve our services and user experience
- Maintain security and prevent fraud

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored in **Supabase** (PostgreSQL) with bank-grade encryption
- Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
- Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensure data isolation between tenants
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt via Supabase Auth)
- JWT tokens are used for session management with automatic expiration

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **NOT** sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Your tenant/business owner**: Technicians and business owners within your service network can see relevant job details
- **Service providers**: Supabase (database hosting), Expo (push notifications)
- **Legal requirements**: If required by law, regulation, or legal process

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information via your profile
- **Deletion**: Request account deletion via Settings → Delete Account
- **Opt-out**: Disable push notifications via Settings
- **Data Portability**: Request your data in a machine-readable format

## 7. Data Retention

- Active account data: Retained while your account is active
- Soft-deleted records: Retained for 90 days before permanent removal
- Audit trails: Retained indefinitely for compliance purposes
- After account deletion: Personal data removed within 30 days

## 8. Children's Privacy

Revivix is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and sending a push notification.
