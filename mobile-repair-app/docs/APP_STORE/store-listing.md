## Full Description

### 📱 Your Device Deserves the Best Care

Revivix connects you with certified mobile repair professionals for fast, reliable device repairs. Whether it's a cracked screen, dying battery, or water damage — we've got you covered.

### ✨ Key Features

**For Customers:**
• Book repairs in minutes with our 4-step wizard
• Choose home visit or store drop-off
• Track your repair status in real-time
• Receive push notifications at every stage
• View transparent, itemized invoices
• Rate and review your technician

**For Technicians:**
• Manage your job queue efficiently
• On-spot repair or shop repair workflows
• Build dynamic invoices with parts, labor & dispatch
• Digital receiving notes for device custody
• Track daily earnings and weekly performance

**For Business Owners:**
• Real-time revenue analytics dashboard
• Manage your technician team
• Monitor all jobs and completion rates
• Revenue breakdown by repair category

### 🔒 Security You Can Trust
• Bank-grade encryption for all data
• JWT-based authentication
• Role-based access control
• Your data is never sold or shared

### 🚀 Getting Started
1. Download and create your account
2. Select your role (Customer, Technician, or Business)
3. Start booking or managing repairs!
