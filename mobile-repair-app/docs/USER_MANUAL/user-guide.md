## Welcome to Revivix 📱

Revivix is a premium mobile repair platform that connects you with certified repair technicians. Whether you need a screen replaced, a battery swapped, or software fixed — we've got you covered.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
1. Open the app and tap **"Create Account"**
2. Select your role (Customer, Technician, or Business)
3. Fill in your name, email, phone, and password
4. Tap **"Create Account"** to register
5. Sign in with your new credentials

### 2. Sign In
1. Enter your email and password
2. Tap **"Sign In"**
3. You'll be taken to your role-specific dashboard

### 3. Forgot Password?
1. On the login screen, tap **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for the reset link
4. Follow the link to set a new password

## For Customers

### Booking a Repair
1. From the home screen, tap **"Book a Repair"**
2. **Step 1 — Device**: Enter your device brand and model
3. **Step 2 — Issue**: Select the type of problem (screen, battery, software, etc.)
4. **Step 3 — Details**: Describe the issue and add photos
5. **Step 4 — Service**: Choose home visit or store drop-off
6. Tap **"Submit Booking"**

### Tracking Your Repair
Your repair goes through these stages:
- 🟡 **Pending** — Awaiting technician assignment
- 🔵 **Assigned** — A technician has been assigned
- 🔵 **En Route** — Technician is on the way
- 🟡 **Diagnosing** — Device is being inspected
- 🔵 **Repairing** — Repair is in progress
- 🟢 **Completed** — Repair is done!

### Viewing Invoices
- Navigate to the **Invoices** tab
- Each invoice shows the device, total, and payment status
- Paid invoices are locked and cannot be modified

### Settings
- Access from the bottom navigation
- Toggle push notifications
- Update your profile
- View privacy policy and terms
- Contact support

## For Technicians

### Managing Jobs
- Your dashboard shows active jobs assigned to you
- Tap any job to view details and begin assessment

### Assessment & Repair Path
After diagnosing, choose your repair path:
- **Path A — On-Spot Fix**: Complete the repair at the customer's location and generate an invoice immediately
- **Path B — Shop Repair**: Create a receiving note documenting the device condition, then take it to the shop

### Building Invoices
1. Add line items (parts, labor, dispatch charges)
2. Remove items if needed
3. Invoice totals auto-calculate with 8% tax
4. When payment is received, tap **"Lock & Send"**

> ⚠️ **Important**: Once locked, an invoice cannot be edited. This is permanent.

## Push Notifications

Revivix sends push notifications for:
- 🔔 Job status changes (assigned, en route, diagnosing, completed)
- 💳 Invoice events (created, payment confirmed)

**To manage notifications:**
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Toggle notification preferences on/off
